Precision Agriculture

Precision Agriculture is a technology-driven approach to farming that utilizes monitoring systems and IoT devices within a connected network to collect and process data for optimized agricultural management. The network architecture varies based on the specific application. This work first explores the components of such a system in detail, then models it within a specific real-world scenario. References are provided throughout and compiled at the end.
